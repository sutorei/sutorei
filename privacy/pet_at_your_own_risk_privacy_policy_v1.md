Privacy Policy for Pet At Your Own Risk

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pet At Your Own Risk handles privacy when you use the mobile game published by Maksim Khaikin.

Pet At Your Own Risk is a mobile game. The app does not require users to create an account with us, and we do not operate our own user database or game server.

This version of the app does not show third-party advertisements and does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

The app may use platform services for leaderboards and in-app purchases. These services are provided by Apple or Google and may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform terms.
